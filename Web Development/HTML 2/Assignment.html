About
Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology is established by Maharaja Agrasen Technical Education Society promoted by a group of well known Industrialists, Businessman, Professionals and Philanthropists with an aim to promote quality education in the field of Technology. MAIT endeavors to provide industry relevant education and training to its well-crafted practical training programs for the student in different semesters of the course. This College represents integrety among students and we want the best for your child as we are a Institute for non profit organisations.
